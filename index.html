About Me

Hello, I'm Caleb. My passion for graphic design all started with my love for anime. In 2018 I was scrolling Instagram and came across a post which was an anime character but edited to look as though they were wearing brand names.

This had peaked my interest and started my journey into the graphic arts world. Since then I have created various anime related projects and shared them with the community that inspired me.
